Who We Are

Welcome to our hotel Hyatt! Our mission is to provide exceptional fitness facilities and personalized training services that cater to your wellness needs.

In a contemporary building next to Magneto The Mall shopping centre, this upscale hotel is 9 km from Swami Vivekananda International Airport. Warmly furnished rooms feature free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and iPod docks, as well as desks, minibars, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Suites add separate living rooms and DVD players. Room service is available 24/7. An informal restaurant serves international fare, and has floor-to-ceiling windows. There's also a low-key bar, a 24-hour fitness room, and a ballroom for up to 600 guests.
